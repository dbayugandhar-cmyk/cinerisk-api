## 1. Data Collection Methodology

CINEOS collects intelligence exclusively from:
- Public Telegram channels (t.me/username format, no login required)
- Public social media posts (Instagram, YouTube, Facebook public pages)
- Public news articles and enforcement press releases
- Public domain registration records (WHOIS)
- Public court records and ED/police press releases

**No private channels, no intercepted communications, no unauthorized access.**

Legal basis: IT Act 2000 §43 excludes publicly accessible information.
