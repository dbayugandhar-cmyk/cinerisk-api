The problem

YOUR RELEASE STRATEGY
IS LEAKING Revenue

01
Staggered releases are the #1 piracy driver
Every day you delay a territory is a day someone in that market turns to piracy. India, China, and Brazil are the highest-risk markets — and most releases stagger them by 3–4 weeks.
02
The leak spreads globally within 48 hours
A cam rip in one territory doesn't stay there. Within 48 hours it's on every major torrent and streaming piracy platform worldwide, undermining your theatrical window everywhere.
03
You're making release decisions without data
Most distribution teams still rely on gut instinct and historical precedent to set release windows. There's no standardised model that tells you what a strategy costs in real money.
04
By the time you react, it's too late
Piracy response is reactive — you find out after the leak. CineRisk gives you the risk profile before you commit to a strategy, when you can still change it.
