📺 What is OTT Piracy?
OTT piracy occurs when subscribers share login credentials or download content and redistribute it on Telegram channels. A single channel with 100,000 subscribers sharing Netflix credentials represents ₹2.99 crore/month in lost subscription revenue. CINEOS monitors 135+ Telegram channels for OTT content leaks across Netflix, Amazon Prime, AHA, ZEE5, SonyLIV and JioHotstar.
