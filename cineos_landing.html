Methodology

Public data.
Rigorous standard.

No private messages. No privacy violations. Every claim on this page is defensible in court.

01

Public Telegram channels only

All data from publicly accessible channels. No private messages. No privacy laws violated. Same access any user has — automated at scale across 1,245 channels daily.

02

SHA-256 hash at moment of capture

Every finding hashed at detection with timestamp and source URL. Chain of custody preserved. Unalterable evidence record per IT Act §65B.

03

Three-tier accuracy model

Tier 1 (95%+) for law enforcement. Tier 2 (85–90%) for intelligence reports. Tier 3 queued until verified. No finding shared until it passes all filters.

04

STIX 2.1 export format

1,138 STIX objects ready. Compatible with CERT-In, I4C and international law enforcement systems.
