Common questions

How quickly do reports arrive?
Brand Protection clients receive a report every Monday at 9am IST automatically. Fraud Intelligence clients receive daily digests and real-time alerts within minutes of detection.
Is the data legally admissible in court?
Yes. All evidence is SHA-256 hashed, HMAC signed and timestamped per IT Act 2000 Section 65B. Evidence packages are accepted for SEBI SCORES complaints, GST Council reports and police FIRs.
What platforms do you monitor?
Telegram (1,077 channels), IndiaMART, Meesho, Instagram, WhatsApp, Reddit, Discord, YouTube, Twitter/X, ShareChat, App Store, dark web paste sites and 103+ piracy websites. All in 6 Indian languages.
