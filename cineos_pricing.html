Common questions

How quickly do reports arrive?
Brand Protection clients receive a report every Monday morning by 9am IST automatically. Fraud Intelligence clients receive daily digests and real-time alerts within minutes of detection.
Is the data legally admissible?
Yes. All evidence is SHA-256 hashed, HMAC signed and timestamped in compliance with IT Act 2000 Section 65B. Evidence packages are accepted for SEBI SCORES complaints, GST Council reports and police FIRs.
What platforms do you monitor?
Telegram (354 channels), IndiaMART, Meesho, Instagram, WhatsApp, Reddit, Discord, dark web paste sites and 103+ piracy websites. All in English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada and Malayalam.
How is pricing structured?
Annual contracts paid quarterly or annually. Brand Protection is per brand — if you have 3 brands (e.g. Dove, Dettol, Surf), that is 3 × Rs 10L = Rs 30L/year. Government and OTT contracts are custom priced based on scope.
What happens in the first week?
Day 1: Onboarding call (30 min). Day 2: First intelligence report delivered. Day 7: Platform access enabled. Week 2: First weekly report. You see real data before the first month invoice.
Can we try before signing a contract?
Yes. We provide a free sample intelligence report specific to your brand before any contract. You see exactly what you are paying for. Email yugandhar@cineos.in to request a sample.
