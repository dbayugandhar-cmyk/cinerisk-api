Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026  ·  Effective: May 10, 2026

Summary: CINEOS is a fraud intelligence platform. We monitor publicly available data on open platforms to detect fraud, counterfeits and financial crime. We do not collect, sell or share personal data of private individuals. We do not use cookies for advertising.

1. Who we are

CINEOS ("we", "our", "us") is India's Trust Intelligence Network, operated by Yugandhar Mallavarapu. CINEOS is protected by US Provisional Patent 64/049,190. Our registered contact address is yugandhar@cineos.in.

CINEOS provides fraud intelligence services to enterprises, financial institutions and government bodies. Our platform detects piracy, counterfeit goods, financial fraud and online scams across 13 digital platforms in India.

2. What data we collect

CINEOS collects two categories of information:

  • Public intelligence data: We monitor publicly accessible channels, listings and posts on platforms including Telegram (public channels), IndiaMART, Meesho, ShareChat, YouTube, Twitter/X, Instagram, WhatsApp (public groups), Discord (public servers), dark web paste sites and app stores. All data collected is from publicly accessible sources.
  • Client contact data: When organisations contact us or subscribe to our services, we collect their name, email address, organisation name and role. This is used solely to deliver our intelligence services and communicate about those services.

We do not collect personal data of fraud victims. We do not collect financial data. We do not collect Aadhaar numbers, PAN numbers or any government identity information about private individuals.

3. What data we do not collect

  • Personal data of private individuals not engaged in fraud
  • Financial account details or payment card information of any person
  • Location data of private individuals
  • Biometric data of any kind
  • Health or medical data
  • Data from minors

4. How we use collected data

Public intelligence data is used exclusively to:

  • Identify and document fraud, counterfeit and piracy activity on digital platforms
  • Generate intelligence reports for enterprise and government clients
  • Support law enforcement and regulatory bodies in India (SEBI, MHA, RBI, TRAI, NPCI, I4C)
  • Improve our fraud detection pattern library and scoring models

Client contact data is used to deliver services, send intelligence reports, and communicate about platform updates. We do not sell client data to third parties.

5. Legal basis for processing

Our collection and use of public intelligence data is grounded in:

  • Legitimate interest: Fraud detection and prevention constitutes a legitimate interest under applicable data protection law. Our monitoring is limited to public channels and is proportionate to the public benefit of fraud detection.
  • Public interest: Intelligence shared with SEBI, MHA, RBI and law enforcement serves the public interest in combating financial crime and consumer fraud.
  • IT Act 2000 compliance: All evidence collected for law enforcement purposes is processed in accordance with Section 65B of the Indian Evidence Act, including SHA-256 hashing and timestamping.

6. India DPDP Act 2023 compliance

CINEOS complies with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. Our data collection is limited to publicly available information. We do not process sensitive personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties outside of law enforcement cooperation under lawful process.

Indian data principals have the right to access information we hold about them and to request correction or erasure. Requests should be sent to yugandhar@cineos.in.

7. GDPR compliance (European partners)

For clients and partners in the European Economic Area, we process contact data under Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract) and Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests). We do not transfer personal data outside India and the EEA without appropriate safeguards.

8. Data retention

  • Intelligence data (public channel findings): Retained for 24 months from collection date, then deleted or anonymised.
  • Client contact data: Retained for the duration of the client relationship plus 3 years.
  • Evidence packages (IT Act 65B): Retained for 7 years in accordance with Indian legal requirements for evidence preservation.

9. Data sharing

We share intelligence data with:

  • Subscribing clients: Enterprise organisations and government bodies that have contracted CINEOS services
  • Indian law enforcement and regulators: SEBI, MHA (I4C), RBI, TRAI, NPCI, state cyber cells — only when legally required or as part of contracted intelligence partnerships
  • Service providers: We use Railway (API hosting), GitHub Pages (website), Google (email) and SerpApi (search data). Each is bound by their own privacy policies.

We do not sell data. We do not share data with advertisers.

10. Security

All intelligence findings are SHA-256 hashed and HMAC-signed before storage or transmission. API access requires authentication keys. Our Railway API is served over HTTPS only. Client emails are transmitted over TLS encryption.

11. Cookies

Our website (cineos.in) does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking. The website is static HTML served via GitHub Pages.

12. Your rights

You have the right to: access data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; object to processing; and lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any right, contact: yugandhar@cineos.in. We will respond within 30 days.
